 \fi 
The mid point of $PB$ is
\begin{align}
\vec{M} &=\frac{1}{2}(\vec{P}+\vec{B})
	= \myvec{4 \\ -2}  
\end{align}
The direction vector of line joining $\vec{O}, \vec{M}$ is 
\begin{align}
\vec{m}&=\vec{O}-\vec{M}
 = -\vec{M}
\end{align}
which can be expressed as
\begin{align}
	\myvec{1 \\ -\frac{1}{2}}
\end{align}
Thus the slope is
\begin{align}
	m = -\frac{1}{2}
\end{align}
